Introduction

This site contains all audio segments that accompany the Touchstone series' Student's Books.

You have three options for listening to the Touchstone audio recordings:

  1. Podcast – click on the RSS / Podcast or iTunes buttons to stream the audio recordings as a Podcast in your media player.
  2. Download – Right-click on the links for individual recordings and choose "Save as" to download the files to your hard drive and then upload in your media player.
  3. In-browser Playing – click on the links for individual recordings to listen to the segments in your web browser.

To begin, choose your Touchstone level from the tabs above.
